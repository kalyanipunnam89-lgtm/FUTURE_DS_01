
# 📊 Business Sales Performance Analytics - Task 1

## 🎯 Objective
To analyze business sales data and identify revenue trends, top-selling products, high-value categories, and regional performance.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (or Power BI / Python if used)

---

## 📈 Analysis Performed
- Revenue trend analysis over time
- Identification of top-selling products
- Analysis of high-value product categories
- Region-wise sales performance analysis
- KPI calculations (sales, revenue, growth)

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Identified products with highest sales contribution
- Found top-performing regions in terms of revenue
- Observed overall sales trend (increase/decrease over time)
- Highlighted high-value categories driving business growth

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Focus on top-performing products to increase revenue
- Improve marketing in low-performing regions
- Optimize inventory for high-demand categories
- Strengthen sales strategy based on trends

---

## 📌 Outcome
- Clear understanding of business performance
- Identified key revenue drivers and weak areas
- Provided actionable insights for business improvement
